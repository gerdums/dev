What we do!

We offer a wide range of marine inspection and training services

Marine Corrosion Surveys

Gladd Solutions provides comprehensive Corrosion Surveys and Inspections for the Marine Environment. From robotic 100% coverage Ultrasonic Hull Plating Side Scans to visual NACE S-CAT Inspections. Gladd Solutions provides the following:

  • US Coast Guard Ultrasonic Hull Plating Side Scans
  • NACE S-CAT inspections
  • ABYC Corrosion Surveys
  • Ultrasonic A-Scan UT readings
  • Insurance Audio gauge readings
  • ABS UT readings (coming soon)
  • Phased Array Inspections

Company Level III Services

You can hire out your Company Level III requirement to Gladd Solutions on an annual contract basis or al-la-carte for Code/Specification compliance with employee certifications and UT inspection procedures. Our Company Level III services include:

  • Contract Company Level III requirement
  • Prepare Company Written Practice per ASNT SNT-TC-1A
  • Establish Employee Certification Plan that complies with Company Written Practice and ASNT SNT-TC-1A
  • Prepare UT inspection procedures to meet Codes, Standards, and Customer Specifications.

Training

Gladd Solutions provides classroom training at customer locations for Ultrasonic and Eddy Current Non-Destructive Testing.

  • Onsite Ultrasonic Level 1 and Level 2
  • Onsite Ultrasonic Limited Level 1 and Level 2, A-Scan Thickness Gauging
  • Onsite Ultrasonic Limited Level 1 and Level 2, AAR Inspection of Axles and Wheels
  • Onsite Eddy Current Level 1 and Level 2
