About us

Plymouth Christian Academy, in partnership with parents, educates students from a biblical worldview and equips them for a lifetime of learning, leadership and service. Our purpose is to produce students matured in their love for God, steeped in His word, educated about His world, dedicated to lives of service and learning, and prepared to fulfill their calling in His kingdom.

PCA offers a unique combination of academic excellence and a family atmosphere, all rooted in the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Caryn Hunstman / Superintendent
